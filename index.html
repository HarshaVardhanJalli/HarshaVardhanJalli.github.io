The opening note.

A brief introduction

I'm a GenAI engineer with twelve years of front-end depth behind me — currently shipping production AI systems for healthcare at Evernorth / Cigna. The interface layer is where most LLM products quietly fail; my edge is knowing both sides.

// THE LATEST CHAPTER

For the last two years, my work has lived squarely inside applied generative AI — production-grade RAG architectures with deliberate chunking strategies and re-ranking layers, multi-agent orchestration through LangGraph and CrewAI, instrumented evaluation pipelines built on LangSmith and Ragas, and the disciplined craft of getting language models to behave predictably inside enterprise products.

At Evernorth (Cigna Healthcare), that translates to GenAI systems operating inside regulated healthcare workflows — where traceability, PHI compliance, and measurable accuracy are foundational, not optional. Prior to this, at LTIMindtree, I designed and shipped "Ask AI" — an OpenAI-powered conversational layer integrated into S&P Global's Platts Dimensions Pro, the firm's flagship commodities-intelligence platform.

Beneath the AI work sits twelve years of front-end engineering across React, Next.js, TypeScript, design systems, and micro-frontend architectures — delivered for JPMorgan, CME, Hitachi, Hexagon, and S&P Global. That foundation is what makes my AI work production-ready: I understand what a streaming token actually looks like in real product UI, not merely in a notebook.

// 2024 — Present · Building With LLMs
